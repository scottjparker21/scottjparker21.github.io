Design & Development

Scott Parker

I am a designer and developer from Milwaukee Wi. I graduated from Milwaukee Area Technical College is the Winter of 2015. After graduating I interned at Lavelle Industries in Burlington for 3 months. After my internship was over and I tried to find a job closer to home. Every job that interested me in the graphic design field was looking for someone with coding experience. So in order to increase my value as a designer I enrolled at devCodeCamp in the part-time program. Now a handful of months later I've graduated with a whole new set of skills and interests. I'm excited to enter the work force with a knowledge of front and backend development as well as the Adobe Creative Suite.

From the first time I used Photoshop my Freshman year of highschool I knew I was hooked. I had always taken art classes and tried my hand at drawing, painting, and ceramics. Digital art appealed to me. I am at home working with Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Flash, and Premier.

Programming is something I never thought I would enjoy. When I first considered learning to code I viewed it as something I had to do in order to get a job in the design world. After coding for a few weeks I realized that I was enjoying solving problems and tackling new challenges. Now as I'm graduating devCodeCamp I can say that coding is one of my passions.
